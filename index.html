Can belly button bacteria be used to create lab grown meat?

Lab grown meat is quickly gaining traction as a meat replacement as the world is learning that current meat and poultry practices are detrimental to the climate. At this point, scientists have only been looking at replicating cells from a single cell in a lab to create meat. However, what is bacteria found in the every day person's belly button could be replicated to create lab grown meat? Data has been collected from participants of the study based on the cleanliness habits, age, gender, the present bacteria in the belly button of each individual, and more to get a clear picture of what sort of bacteria does exist in our belly buttons.
